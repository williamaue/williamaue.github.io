About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Purdue University with Dr. Jeffrey D. Karpicke. My work has largely focused on understanding how information is updated in memory. This has included examining how previous experience influences more recent, novel associations, as well as how encoding during testing both helps and harms memory performance. I address the question using a combination of experimental, statistical, and computational techniques.
